## Hi, I'm Josh

Full-stack **Laravel** developer and consultant with 9+ years building
production web applications. My go-to stack is **Laravel + Filament**,
reaching for **React** and **TypeScript** when the project calls for it.
Infrastructure experience spanning **Kubernetes**, **AWS**, and
**CI/CD pipelines**.

Most of my career has been in **higher education** — building admissions
systems, student information system integrations, and identity
infrastructure across multiple institutions. I've also built a
**HIPAA-compliant patient portal**, a **reading education platform** in
React/TypeScript + Laravel Nova with an OpenAPI-specified backend, and
various tools for small businesses through consulting work.

I care about **healthcare technology** — specifically the idea that people
should own their health data and that providers should own the software
their practices depend on. The
[health dashboard](https://health.joshbowen.net)
is a small step in that direction: a privacy-first PWA where all data
stays on your device.

I'm all in on **agentic engineering** — not vibe coding or spamming open
source with AI slop, but genuinely partnering with agents to architect,
review, and ship. It's changed what I spend my time on: less typing code,
more reading code, designing systems, and doing the work that matters.
